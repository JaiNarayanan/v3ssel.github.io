V3SSEL Leadership Initative

The V3SSEL Leadership Initiative is a 501(c)(3) organization that seeks to foster healthy policy discussions between leaders in a diversity of fields and youth in communities around the globe.

Our Model

Through open forum seminar events at high schools, libraries, and other venues around the world, we bring leaders with years of experience in various fields to discuss important policy issues with youth.

Our model emphasizes back and forth discussion through question and answer periods as well as extensive polling among youth before and after events.

Our Core Values

The 3 in V3SSEL represents our three core values: Leadership, Activism, and Connection.

  • Leadership

    Through enabling rich discussions between leaders and youth, we hope to inspire the next generation of leaders to take on the worldâ€™s most pressing issues.

  • Activism

    Our seminar events give youth the knowledge and confidence to act on their beliefs while understanding differing perspectives.

  • Connection

    Our policy discussions connect youth with leaders in a wide range of fields and bring youth with a diversity of perspectives together in an open forum environment.
